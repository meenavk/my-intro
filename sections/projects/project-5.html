Lean Qualification Bundle Simplification Program

Led the development and implementation of a Lean Qualification Bundle solution to simplify converting line qualification processes, reduce operational losses, and improve startup efficiency through controls innovation, automation, and data-driven interventions.

Lean Manufacturing Process Improvement PLC Controls HMI Development Data Analytics Operational Excellence

Project Overview

Transformed a complex qualification process into a simplified, standardized workflow by developing integrated controls solutions, operator interfaces, data collection systems, and proactive intervention tools. The project progressed from concept to full-scale implementation and delivered measurable improvements in manufacturing performance.

Challenges

  • Complex and time-consuming line qualification activities.
  • High startup losses and extended qualification duration.
  • Significant material waste during qualification runs.
  • Limited operational visibility during qualification processes.
  • Inconsistent execution across production teams.

Solution Delivered

  • Developed simplified qualification logic within machine control systems.
  • Designed operator-friendly HMI interfaces for qualification management.
  • Implemented automated data collection and performance tracking.
  • Created proactive intervention tools to identify issues before product losses occurred.
  • Standardized qualification workflows across production operations.
  • Supported implementation from concept development through production deployment.

Business Impact

  • Reduced qualification-related downtime by approximately 30%.
  • Reduced material waste by approximately 35%.
  • Improved startup efficiency and qualification consistency.
  • Increased operational visibility and faster issue resolution.
  • Enabled sustainable process improvements through standardized controls.
  • Delivered measurable productivity and cost savings benefits.
