Recipe Automation & Centralized Changeover Management System

Developed a Python-based recipe management platform integrated with PLC-controlled manufacturing systems to automate product changeovers, centralize recipe management, and enhance operational flexibility beyond traditional FactoryTalk RPP functionality.

Python PLC Integration Recipe Management FactoryTalk Excel Automation Manufacturing Systems

Project Overview

Replaced traditional FactoryTalk RecipePro Plus (RPP) workflows with a customized Python-based recipe automation solution. The platform enables touchless recipe operations, centralized recipe management, configurable changeover behavior, and enhanced manufacturing flexibility while maintaining safe integration with PLC-controlled production systems.

Before Implementation

  • Manual intervention required during product changeovers.
  • FactoryTalk Studio and RPP recipe files were required for recipe management.
  • Recipe updates had to be maintained separately across multiple files.
  • Limited customization and operational flexibility.
  • Increased risk of recipe version inconsistencies.

Solution Delivered

  • Developed a centralized Python-based recipe management platform.
  • Implemented touchless recipe changeover operations.
  • Enabled Excel-based recipe configuration and maintenance.
  • Introduced centrally managed recipe repositories.
  • Implemented configurable Tag Exclude Sets to retain machine base conditions during product transitions.
  • Added post-recipe execution commands and automated changeover actions.

Business Benefits

  • Reduced operator intervention during product changeovers.
  • Simplified recipe maintenance and version control.
  • Improved consistency across manufacturing lines.
  • Enhanced flexibility through customizable recipe logic.
  • Reduced changeover time and operational complexity.
  • Improved scalability for future product introductions.
