Project Overview

Designed and developed a manufacturing analytics platform that extends the capabilities of PLC-controlled production systems by providing advanced calculations, diagnostics, operational monitoring, and data-driven decision support.

Key Highlights

  • Integrated Python applications with PLC-controlled manufacturing systems.
  • Performed advanced process calculations and manufacturing analytics outside the PLC environment.
  • Developed operational monitoring and troubleshooting tools for engineers and technicians.
  • Improved manufacturing visibility through data collection, processing, analytics, and reporting.
  • Enabled faster troubleshooting and improved operational decision-making through diagnostics and insights.
  • Maintained all critical machine control and real-time execution safely within PLC architectures.
  • Reduced PLC processing load by moving advanced calculations and analytics to the application layer.
  • Supported Digital Manufacturing and Industry 4.0 initiatives through OT/IT integration.
