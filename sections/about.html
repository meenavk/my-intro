About Me

Throughout my career at Procter & Gamble, I have been passionate about helping manufacturing operations achieve higher levels of safety, reliability, and performance through effective automation and control systems.

My experience spans manufacturing controls, industrial automation, machine safety, motion control, industrial networking, and digital manufacturing technologies across high-speed production environments.

I enjoy solving complex manufacturing challenges by combining practical engineering knowledge with modern technologies that support continuous improvement and operational excellence.

I am a Power, Control & Information Systems (PC&IS) professional with more than two decades of experience supporting manufacturing operations within Procter & Gamble. Over the years, I have worked extensively with hygiene, paper, and high-speed converting manufacturing systems, supporting both day-to-day operations and long-term manufacturing improvement initiatives.

My primary focus has always been ensuring safe, reliable, and efficient manufacturing performance through robust automation systems, effective troubleshooting practices, and continuous process improvement.

Throughout my career, I have participated in manufacturing startups, commissioning activities, equipment upgrades, control system modernization projects, process optimization programs, and reliability improvement initiatives across multiple manufacturing technologies.
